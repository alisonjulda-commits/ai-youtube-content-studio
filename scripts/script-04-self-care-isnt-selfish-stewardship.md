## SCRIPT

**[0:00–0:03] HOOK**
*(On-screen text: "Self-care isn't selfish." Bold, center screen.)*

> "If you've ever felt guilty for resting, for saying no, or for taking care of your body — I need you to hear this."

**[0:03–0:12] SCRIPTURE**
*(On-screen text: 1 Corinthians 6:19–20, soft scripture card overlay)*

> "Scripture says your body is a temple of the Holy Spirit — that you were bought at a price. That's not a suggestion to feel guilty about your needs. That's a call to *stewardship*."

**[0:12–0:35] TEACHING / BODY**
*(B-roll: journaling, drinking water, quiet morning routine, closing laptop)*

> "Stewardship means taking care of what God has entrusted to you. Your time. Your energy. Your body. Your peace. When you rest, you're not being lazy — you're maintaining the temple. When you set a boundary, you're not being difficult — you're protecting what God gave you to steward. Self-care rooted in scripture isn't about indulgence. It's about honoring the vessel God is using for His purpose."

**[0:35–0:50] APPLICATION**
*(On-screen text: 3 quick action points appear one by one)*

> "So today, try this: One — say no to something that drains you without guilt. Two — drink the water, take the walk, get the sleep. Three — remind yourself: caring for this body is caring for God's temple."

**[0:50–0:58] CTA / CLOSE**
*(On-screen text: "You are not your own — you were bought at a price.")*

> "You weren't made to run on empty. Follow for more biblical self-care, and drop a 🙏 if you needed this today."
